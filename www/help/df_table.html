Help on: df table

What are df?
df stands for degrees of freedom. Depending on the number of subjects there are a certain amount of degrees of freedom. Each covariable you choose will use up a certain amount of degrees of freedom (numeric variables use one and non-numeric use as many as the number of different values,eg. gender would use two, one for male and one for female).

What is the df table for?
The df table will display the covariables you have selected, the number of degrees of freedom used and the maximum allowed.
