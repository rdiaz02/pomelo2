Help on: Heatmap

What does the heatmap plot show?
The heatmap shows gene expression data for each subject by means of colour intensity.

Why do only a few genes appear or none at all?
The heatmap will only show those genes that have a FDR-value below 0.15 and a unadjusted-p-value below 0.05 with a maximum of 50 genes. If none of your genes fulfill these conditions, then no heatmap will appear. If you wish to change these conditions, you can click on edit heatmap.
