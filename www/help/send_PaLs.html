Help on: Send to PaLS

What is PaLS?
PaLS is a tool that analyzes sets of lists of genes or single lists of genes. It filters those genes/clones/proteins that are referenced by a given percentage of PubMed references, Gene Ontology terms, KEGG pathways or Reactome pathways. For more information check PaLS help.

How do I send a list of genes to PaLS from Pomelo II?
Select the parameters you want and click on "Make gene list". You will get the number of genes that match this criteria and a link to the list of genes. Once you are happy with the list, you can download the list or send it directly to PaLS.
